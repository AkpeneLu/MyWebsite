Francesca Akpene Lumor

I am a Norwegian self-taught artist with a bachelor’s degree in Psychology from the University of Oslo, currently studying Computer Science.
Welcome to my portfolio.
I have been drawing for as long as I can remember but hated it when I was younger, swearing when I grow up I will never pick up a pencil again. Look at me now? Creating art is my passion, which I discovered while studying Arts, Crafts and Design with my General Studies in secondary school. What I love about it the most is seeing how a simple pencil-line on a paper can transform into a beautiful picture. Now, I want to showcase my art to others through my own website.
Pencils are my tools of choice, both colour and graphite. I also like to try out things outside of my comfort zone, so I also paint sometimes using oil, acrylic and watercolours.
A great piece of artwork captures the feelings of the moment, and I do my best to accomplish just that.
